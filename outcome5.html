Class: Business architecture

Project: Agile Project methology

In business Architecture we learned a tool that helps in the development cycle, it called Agile. Agile allows flexibility during the project cycle. If the client wants to change a requirement it shouldnâ€™t be a huge deal if agile has been applied properly. It forces people and developers to work closer together on a given project. This allows for face-to-face interactions with your team members, the most efficient way to convey information.

We used Agile in all of our major projects in our Business architecture class. We start by picking roles in our team. You can be a team member, scrum master , or product owner. The scrum master is the lead in the four daily meeting. They are in charge of keeping the meetings short but informative. Anytime a team member finishes an item on a sprint they must articulate what they have done to the team so that we are all on the same page. This is especially important when it comes to software. You need to be able to leave identifying comment in your code and explain it to anyone without a problem.

This process taught me that team work can be very productive if the right steps are taken. Agile is big part of the developing industry and it will only help if I learn it thoroughly.

A short term goal is to learn Agile and all of its foundational properties. Companies are relaying on Agile now more than ever before, and it would benefit me to learn it by heart.One Long term goal I have set for myself is to eventually become a project manager for a software department.
